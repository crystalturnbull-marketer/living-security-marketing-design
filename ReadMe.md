# Living Security Brand Guidelines

This repository is the source of truth for the Living Security brand — our voice, our visual identity, and the standards that make us recognizable across every touchpoint.

Whether you're building a deck, writing a campaign, designing an asset, or training an AI tool on our brand, start here.

---

## What's Inside

**[LivingSecurity_BrandGuidelines_2026.md](./LivingSecurity_BrandGuidelines_2026.md)**

The full brand guide, covering:

- Brand Story & Livvy Introduction
- Mission, Vision & Core Values
- Voice & Tone — how we write and what we avoid
- Logo usage — primary, secondary, icon, and clear space rules
- Typography — Inter Tight, Arial, and accent fonts
- Color palettes — Primary, Secondary, and Livvy
- The Aesthetic — textures, iconography, photo direction
- Accessibility contrast standards (WCAG 2.1)
